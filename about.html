About Our Community

We preserve Mon heritage through youth leadership, cultural education, and traditional dance.

Our Mission

Mon Youth Community of Colorado exists to empower young people, celebrate our heritage, and strengthen our community through cultural expression.


Through dance, music, mentorship, and shared experiences, we help the next generation stay connected to Mon identity while growing in confidence and leadership.
