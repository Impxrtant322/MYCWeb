Spotlight Performers

Meet the talented young individuals who bring our cultural stories to life on stage.

Laya Roon

Specializing in classical Mon dances for over 8 years, Laya leads our youth ensemble with grace.

Aindra Mon

Balancing performance with mentorship, Aindra helps our youngest members learn their first steps.

Kong Tala

A vibrant performer of Mon folk traditions, Kong brings high energy and storytelling to every event.

Htaw Poine

Lead Guitarist of the MYC Band, Poine plays with dignity and grace to bring excitement to the show.
